(K3) Bij Ons Thuis

 

Bij Ons Thuis is a K3 song.

 

Category: party

Rating: 1/10

Favorite lyric: none

 

In this song, K3 describes having a party. The song has a cheerful verse and a cheerful chorus. There is an intermezzo with jabbertalk.

 

Personally, I feel this song has nothing to say, contains no jokes nor wit.
