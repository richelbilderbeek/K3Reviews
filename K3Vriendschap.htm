(K3) Vriendschap

 

Vriendschap is a K3 song.

 

Category: ethics

Rating: 6/10

Favorite lyric: 'Wees altijd jezelf, want voor ons ben je een held, als je iedereen je bonzend hart laat zien'

 

In this song, K3 sing about the benefits of friendship. The song has two moods: neutral verses and a neutral chorus.

 

Personally, except for the favorite lyric ('Always be yourself, because for us you are a hero, if you show your (nervously) beating heart to everyone) the song is mediocre and plain.
