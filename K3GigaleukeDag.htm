Category: party

Rating: 4/10

Favorite lyric: none

 

In this song, K3 describes that today will be a fun day. The song has two moods: neutral verses and neutral chorus.

 

Personally, I find this one of the many uninspired party songs. Both [1] and [2] are expressions used too commonly in other uninspired party songs as well. As in many party songs, it mentioned that today will be fun, leaving some more interesting questions open: why is this day special and how to act to make it so? The only hints given are that it is a costume party (but why this party? Why costumes? Must the costumes have a theme?) and that they eat sugar waffles (why sugar waffles? Is this the main fun activity? What does this have to do with the costume party?). I get the feeling all these questions I have are caused by forced rhyming instead of an idea behind the song. For me, the melody does not enhance my party mood, which is in my opinion fitting: the melody is as uninspired as the lyrics, so they fit together great.
