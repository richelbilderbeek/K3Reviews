Category: party

Rating: 9/10

Favorite lyric: 'dan zou je denken dat de wereld voor het eerst een dag vrijaf heeft'

 

This song describes the onset of summer holidays and the party that evening

 

I think the song has a very strong beginning, building up from classroom to outside, where the feeling of happiness comes to full bloom. After this, the song has a melody I find enjoyable. Thanks to the strong onset of the song, I don't mind the rest of the song being just a normal party song. I don't like that school is mentioned as being nonsensical [1], or Kathleen stating that working is nothing for her [2] (I think this is not true, and I do think K3 should not sing about being lazy). The rhyme of [4] is, in my opinion, a bit cheap. I really like Karen singing my favorite sentence [4].
