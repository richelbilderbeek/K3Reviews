In this song, K3 describes the many musical instruments (and their sounds) of a party, who is present at the party, and how the girls behave to improve the party its atmosphere.

 

Personally, I think the lyrics are rather moronic: singing 'ja ja jajaja' [1] is, in my humble opinion, not very eloquent. Chanting the sounds musical instruments make could be nice educational material in kindergarden, but it decreases the philosophical weight of the song. Except for these two points of critique, I do like the music itself. Additionally, when Karen sings 'Elke jongen kiest een leuke meid' [3], I wish I was there to ask Karen to dance with me.
