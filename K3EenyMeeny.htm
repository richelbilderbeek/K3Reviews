(K3) Eeny Meeny

 

Eeny Meeny is a K3 song.

 

Category: love

Rating: 4/10

Favorite lyric: 'Ik hou van Bert, maar ik houd ook van Jan'

 

In this song, K3 sing about being in love with two men, and unable to choose, they choose one at random. The song has two moods: neutral verses and a cheerful chorus.

 

Personally, I dislike the lyrics for pursueing monogamy (as the girls have to choose), instead of considering a more liberal relationship. I think my favorite lyric should have been 'Ik love Gert, but I also love Sam(son)'.
