(K3) Feestje

 

Feestje is a K3 song.

 

Category: party

Rating: 5/10

Favorite lyric: 'Op naar het stort met die gezellige stoel en het bed in de fik, want wij gaan nooit meer slapen'

 

In this song, K3 sing about organizing a party. The song has two moods: neutral verses and a neutral chorus.

 

Personally, I like lyrics to actually describe something more than just partying. My favorite lyric ("To the landfill with the comfortable chair and set the bed on fire, because we will never sleep again") is the only reason for me to listen to this song: I enjoy these forceful statements!
