(K3) Sterren

 

Sterren is a K3 song.

 

Category: love

Rating: 7/10

Favorite lyric: none

 

In this song, K3 describes having fun activities with a boyfriend and the sensation of kissing him. The song has a neutral verse, a cheerful chorus and a more sensitive verse in which K3 phantasizes about marriage.

 

Personally, I enjoy this song as I like the cheerful activities of K3 and the boy, as well as the romance of them kissing. Mentioning celestial objects is also a plus for me.
